About

Where graphic design meets laser precision. A designer first, and a maker by hand second, or maybe it is the other way round.

For over two decades, Remon has worked across graphic design and the craft of laser cutting and engraving, building a practice where a concept does not stop at the screen. It gets tested in wood, refined under the laser head, and finished by hand until the grain, the light and the message all sit right together.

That range is what shapes the work. A trained eye for layout, type and composition, paired with real technical command of laser settings, materials and finishing. The result is pieces that read as considered design objects, not just cutouts: personalised signs, keepsakes, keychains and décor made to be kept, not just looked at.

Every commission, big or small, gets the same attention from the first sketch to the final polish.
